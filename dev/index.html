createsubsystemfrombuses(sysfull::PSY.System, subsystembusnumbers::Vector{Int64}) -Takes a deepcopy of PSY system and removes all components that are not attached to the buses included in subsystem_bus_numbers. -Leaves all branches that connect two buses within the surrogate AND branches that connect to the rest of the system ("connecting buses") -Adds source components at connecting buses –- these are used to perturb the surrogate when generating training data.
